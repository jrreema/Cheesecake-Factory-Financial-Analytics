# Cheesecake Factory Financial Analytics Dashboard
Financial Analytics & KPI Performance Review (2018–2022)
## Project Overview

This project analyzes The Cheesecake Factory’s financial performance from 2018–2022 using Excel and Power BI dashboards. The analysis focuses on profitability, liquidity, leverage, shareholder returns, and operational efficiency through KPI-driven reporting and business intelligence dashboards.

---

## Business Objective

Evaluate financial performance trends and identify key business insights related to:

* Profitability
* Liquidity
* Debt Management
* Shareholder Returns
* Operational Efficiency

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Financial Ratio Analysis
* KPI Reporting
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence

---

## Key KPIs Analyzed

* Return on Equity (ROE)
* Return on Assets (ROA)
* Current Ratio
* Debt-to-Equity Ratio
* Asset Turnover Ratio
* Earnings Per Share (EPS)

---

## Key Insights

* Profitability declined significantly during 2020 and recovered steadily post-pandemic
* Debt leverage increased consistently from 2018–2022
* Liquidity remained below ideal benchmark levels
* Revenue and operational performance improved after 2021
* Operational efficiency strengthened gradually after pandemic disruptions

---

## Recommendations

* Reduce reliance on debt financing
* Strengthen liquidity management strategies
* Improve operational efficiency through optimized asset utilization
* Continue KPI-driven financial performance monitoring
